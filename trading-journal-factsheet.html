Core Capabilities
📡
AI Call Analyzer
Paste any analyst call — Claude extracts entry/SL/TP, scores 1-10, checks ATR noise, portfolio correlation, and your personal rulebook. Links call to Bitget or Blofin position.
⭐
Setup Scanner
Scans 100 USDT-M symbols every 30 min. 3-stage pipeline: confluence → quality gate → AI scoring. Sends Telegram alerts for 6+/10 setups with entry/SL/TP.
⚡
Live Trade AI
Real-time AI on every open position across Bitget & Blofin: risk rating, Hold/Close/Adjust recommendation, SL/TP suggestions, historical context.
🔮
Hindsight Analysis
Retroactively scores your last 50-100 trades using historical candles. Shows how your P&L would differ following AI recommendations — filterable by exchange.
🔬
Edge Lab
Self-learning Trader Rulebook from your own trade history. Pattern detector finds win/loss patterns by setup, session, weekday, and direction — per exchange or combined.
📊
Chart Explorer
Any symbol, any timeframe. VMC Cipher B oscillator, auto-Fibonacci retracements, weekly S/R zones, volume histogram, ATR-adaptive trendlines with at-risk flagging.
