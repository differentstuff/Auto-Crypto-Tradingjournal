R:R Ratio

R:R 1:2.5 means: for every $1 you risk (entry → SL), you stand to gain $2.50 (entry → TP1). Minimum recommended: 2:1. Below 1.5:1 is not worth the risk.

DCA (Dollar-Cost Average)

Entering in two tranches at different prices to get a lower average entry. First entry at the current level, second (DCA) entry lower if price dips. Risk is 2% with DCA vs 1% for a single entry.

⚠ Warnings Explained
⚠ ATR Risk

ATR = Average True Range — the typical candle-to-candle price movement. If your SL is inside the ATR noise (< 1× ATR from entry), normal price fluctuation may stop you out before the trade has a chance to work. Move the SL further away or skip the trade.

⚠ Portfolio Correlation

You already have open positions in the same sector and same direction. If BTC dumps or the sector rotates, all those positions move against you at the same time. Consider reducing size or waiting for one to close first.

⚠ Candle-Close Stop Loss

This SL only triggers when a full candle closes below/above the level — not on a wick touch. It's more forgiving of noise, but Bitget cannot automate this. You must watch the chart and close manually when the candle closes through the level.

⚠ Personal Pattern Warnings

These are based on your own trade history — recurring situations where you specifically tend to lose. Not generic advice. If it says "Friday trades lose" that means YOUR Fridays, not the market in general.
